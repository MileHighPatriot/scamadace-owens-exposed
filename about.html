About this project

Scamdace Owens Exposed

A public, static research site that catalogs Candace Owens’s claims about Charlie Kirk’s assassination and answers them with the public evidence record.

Author

MileHigh Patriot on X: @America1st5280

Mission

After Charlie Kirk was murdered, a marketplace of theories exploded. Owens became one of the most prolific amplifiers — often packaging implication, foreign-villain rotation, and attacks on people around Kirk as “investigation.” This site exists so ordinary people can equip themselves with organized facts when confronting that content online or in public.

What we are / are not

  • Pro Charlie Kirk’s memory, Erika Kirk, and the Kirk family.
  • Anti false assassination narratives and the culture that treats Owens’s every pivot as revelation.
  • Not affiliated with TPUSA, Candace Owens, any campaign, or any government.
  • Not a doxxing project. No attacks on private non-public persons’ personal data.
  • Not a venue for personal insults. We do describe false claims as false and dishonest methods as dishonest.

Standards

  • Claims first, then disproof (so readers recognize what they heard on her show).
  • Primary Owens sources preferred; secondary only when quoting her supportively/neutrally as documentation of what she said.
  • Evidence hierarchy published on every deep-dive.
  • Unknowns labeled as unknowns — never as proof of a preferred theory.
  • Coverage window for the assassination catalog: Sept 10, 2025 – July 19, 2026 (launch baseline), expandable via submissions.
